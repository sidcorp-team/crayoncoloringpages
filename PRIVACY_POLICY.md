# Privacy Policy for Crayon Coloring Pages

**Last Updated:** November 10, 2025

## Introduction

Crayon Coloring Pages ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

**App Developer:** Dunkare
**App Name:** Crayon Coloring Pages
**Package ID:** com.dunkare.crayoncoloringpages

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **Photos and Images**: When you use the camera feature or select photos from your gallery to create coloring pages
- **Created Artwork**: Your colored images that you choose to save to your device

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (used for app functionality and security)
- **Usage Data**: App features you use and interactions within the app (stored locally only)

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
- ❌ We do NOT track your location
- ❌ We do NOT share your data with third parties for advertising
- ❌ We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

1. **App Functionality**
   - Downloading coloring page images from our content server
   - Converting your photos into coloring pages using camera or gallery
   - Saving your colored artwork to your device's photo gallery
   - Providing offline access to previously downloaded content

2. **Security**
   - Generating device-specific signatures to authenticate requests to our server
   - Preventing abuse and ensuring fair usage of our content delivery system

3. **App Improvement**
   - Understanding which features are most used (data stored locally only)
   - Improving app performance and user experience

## Permissions Explained

Our app requests the following permissions:

### Internet Access
**Why:** To download coloring page images and content from our server
**What we access:** Only our own content delivery server
**Data sent:** Device fingerprint (for authentication), no personal data

### Camera
**Why:** To take photos that you can turn into custom coloring pages
**What we access:** Camera only when you tap the camera button
**Data storage:** Photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers

### Read Media Images / Read External Storage
**Why:** To let you select photos from your gallery to create coloring pages
**What we access:** Only images you explicitly select
**Data storage:** Selected images are processed locally, never uploaded

### Write External Storage / Media Images
**Why:** To save your completed colored artwork to your device's photo gallery
**What we access:** Only the "Crayon Coloring Pages" album in your gallery
**Data storage:** Images are saved locally on your device only

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All your created artwork is stored **only on your device**
- Downloaded coloring pages are cached locally for offline access
- No artwork or photos are uploaded to our servers

### Server Communication
When the app connects to our content server:
- **What is sent:** Device fingerprint (anonymous identifier), request timestamp
- **What is received:** Coloring page images and content metadata
- **Encryption:** All communications use HTTPS encryption
- **No personal data:** We never send or store personal information

### Data Security
- Device identifiers are hashed using SHA-256 encryption
- Server requests use HMAC signatures to prevent tampering
- No user accounts or login required
- No cloud storage of user data

## Third-Party Services

Our app **does NOT use**:
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- ❌ Social media integrations
- ❌ Third-party tracking

Our app **DOES use**:
- ✅ Our own content delivery server (for downloading coloring pages)
- ✅ Standard Flutter framework libraries
- ✅ Local device storage only

- ✅ We do NOT share data with third parties

### Parental Controls

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access
Since we don't collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no data to access or download.

### Data Deletion
- **Artwork:** Delete your saved artwork directly from your device's photo gallery
- **App Data:** Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
- **Server Data:** We only store anonymous device fingerprints for security; contact us to request deletion

### Permissions Control
You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings:
- **Android:** Settings → Apps → Crayon Coloring Pages → Permissions
